UG Project- Autonomous Agricultural Robot (TEQIP III Sponsored)

Dhruv Patel, Meet Gandhi, Shankaranarayanan H

Oct 2019 – June 2020 NIT Surat

Advisor : Dr. Anand Darji (Associate Professor & Former HoD, ECED)

Worked on a Robot that autonomously navigate through the fields and is able to perform vision task - Crop Weed Classification which is a major problem in the field of farming.

I mainly worked on Deep Learning part where I implemented and tested 2 segmentation models: UNet and Bonnet for Crop Weed Classification task. Final Bonnet model achieved 96.48% accuracy and 0.0168 units loss on CWFID whereas 99.471% mean accuracy, 98.035% mean iou and loss of 0.0035 units on Bonn dataset. Real-time latency of model is avg. 2.5 fps on i7 + NVIDIA 940MX which makes it possible to deploy for real-case scenarios.

PROJECT PAGE
Keywords: Robotics, Deep Learning, Sensor fusion, Autonomous Navigation, NVIDIA Jetson Nano.
